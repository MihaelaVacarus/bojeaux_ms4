About

Bojeaux is a mature skin and hair care brand that entered the market back in 2019. Reinventing the world of luxury through a new approach where design meets high quality, our products line started in the Amsterdam, Netherlands. Born in such a vibrant and international city, Bojeaux delivers with elegance and sophistication by enhancing your natural beauty and taking care of you.

Our founders

Berhane Oghenekevwe

Born in Kampala (Uganda), Berhane moved to the Netherlands with her parents when she was six years old. Growing up she has always shown interest in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics, so she decided to pursue a career as a cosmetic scientist. This passion eventually led to Berhane starting her first line of skin care products, with a focus on high quality ingredients that would make an impact in the industry.

Phương Lan and Thea Jasmin

Phương and Thea, both born and raised in Berlin (Germany), moved to the Netherlands back in 2018, right after finishing college. Business graduates with a great interest in starting a company focused on hair care products within the high-end market, they met Berhane at a marketing event and everything started to take shape. Shortly thereafter, the first line of skin care products was launched successfully and has grown ever since, incorporating more and more products.
