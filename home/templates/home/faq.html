Frequently Asked Questions

We ship your order anywhere in the world within 3 days. Bojeaux orders are shipped and delivered by UPS on business days (Monday through Friday, excluding major holidays). Time frames could take between 4-10 business days and shipping costs are calculated based on your order total (if you spend a minimum of $45, you get free delivery!).

Returns can be sent up to 90 days after receiving your order. However, please note that our products can only be returned if sealed and unopened, due to hygiene. Please return your products in the original shipping box to and use the sticker provided in the package you receive from us.

We are available Mon to Fri on our social media channels. For any questions regarding your order, you can contact us on Instagram, Facebook or Twitter and our customer service team will get back to you as soon as possible.

Generally, all cosmetic products have a shelf life of at least 2 years, if the packaging is sealed. On the packaging of your specific product, you can find an icon (a number with the letter 'M') that indicates how many months the quality can be guaranteed after opening. In case this icon is not visible on your product, then you can store it for at least two years in a cool, dry, and dark environment.

Our experts are ready to answer your questions and help you find the best products for you. Reach out to us on social media and we will guide you to find the best matches for you.

First of all, please contact a doctor immediately for advice on any allergic reactions. Even though our products have been carefully tested, it could happen that your skin may react unexpectedly to one of our ingredients. In those cases, we advise you to stop using the product and give us all the product information (keep it!), so we can further investigate.

Currently, our products are only available online. We are working hard to make our products available in stores as well, so make sure to follow us for any updates on that!
